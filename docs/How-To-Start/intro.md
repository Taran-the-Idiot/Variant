# Variant

Welcome to Variant! The ysws where you make a hardware project and give it a unique gimmick!

## The Gist

In this ysws, you can take any hardware project(keyboards, 3d printers, devboards, etc) and give them a unique gimmick! Some examples include the [Foldboard](https://github.com/Taran-the-Idiot/Fold-Board), [Choc3DP](https://github.com/SamhithPola2025/Choc3DP), third example.

Your gimmick needs to be unique so you can't make things like a keyboard that is split into 2 parts(just a split keeb), but more niche ideas like detachable compartments, 3D printers that print food, or something else that your imagination comes up with.
