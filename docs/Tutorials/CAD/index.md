# Intro to CAD

CAD(computer aided design) is the use of computers to aid in the creation of a design(wikipedia definition).

This section is a range of guides on how to do different things on CAD software. If there is anything you can't find here, I suggest for you to go onto youtube to learn it from a video there. THere are lots of good tutorials available and they help out a lot.

## What Software do I use?

There are heaps of different software available but I will chalk it down to 3 that I reccomend based on your preference.

1. Shapr3D - Shapr3d is free for all students with a school email and I reccomend it to anyone who is on Macos. You can download and apply for an education license [here](https://www.shapr3d.com/education)
2. Onshape - Onshape is a browser based CAD Software that is free for non-comercial use. I recommend it to anyone on Linux or anyone that uses a weak device. You can sign up [here](https://www.onshape.com/en/products/free)
3. Fusion 360 - Fusion gets a lot of hate and a lot of love depending on who you ask. I personally haven't used it but I am including it on this list for anyone who wants to use it. Not recommended for anyone on intel mac. You can sign up [here](https://www.autodesk.com/products/fusion-360/personal)

If you are on windows then you can pick any of those 3. Yall are blessed with options.
