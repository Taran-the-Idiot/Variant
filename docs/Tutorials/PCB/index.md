# What is this section?

PCBs(Printed Circuit Boards) are the backbone of society. Nearly every electronic device around you uses them in one form or another. This section teaches you skills to put into making your own pcbs.


## What Software should I use?

Kicad, without question, forever in a million years Kicad.

Kicad is the best PCB design option available for beginners and people in general(argueable but I stand by it). Kicad is free, open-source and has hundreds of tutorials available throughout the web.

You can download it [here](https://www.kicad.org/).

Kicad works on all operating systems and is lightweight and can run on most devices(even some as old as 2015)
